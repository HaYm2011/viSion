viSion.

An Object Memory Ambient Assistant

An ambient AI assistant designed to track the location of your everyday objects, log habits, and answer queries through voice interaction.

Core Capabilities

Object Permanence

viSion tracks object position changes. For example: "Keys appeared at 2:32 PM on the counter".

Habit Logging

Automatically log your habits and routines, helping you stay on track with your goals.

Voice Interaction

Ask questions like "Where is my wallet?" and get instant answers.

Voice Interaction Demo

Beyond Object Tracking

Diet & Nutrition

Keep a visual diary of your meals. Get insights into your eating habits and nutritional intake.

Smart Reminders

Set reminders for medication, appointments, or any other important event, and viSion will remember and remind you.
